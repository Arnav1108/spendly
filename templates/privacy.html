Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided during registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter when logging expenses.
  • Usage data: basic information about how you interact with the app, such as pages visited and actions taken, used solely to improve the service.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To authenticate you and display your personal expense records.
  • To generate summaries, category breakdowns, and other insights within your account.
  • To respond to support requests or questions you send us.
  • To identify and fix bugs or performance issues in the application.

We will never use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the expense tracking service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

Your expense data is linked to your account and is not accessible to other users. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the address below — we will process such requests within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. We may use a limited set of trusted third-party services solely to operate the application (for example, hosting infrastructure). These providers are contractually required to keep your data confidential and may not use it for their own purposes.

We use Google Fonts to load typefaces. This causes your browser to make a request to Google's servers when you visit Spendly. No personal expense data is included in these requests. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle font loading requests.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in during your browsing session. This cookie contains no personal information and is deleted when you log out or close your browser. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request access to or deletion of your data, please contact us at privacy@spendly.app.
